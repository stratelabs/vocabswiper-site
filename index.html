Questions, answered.

Which languages can I learn?
Twenty-two and counting: Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Dutch, Turkish, Polish, Swedish, Danish, Finnish, Greek, Czech, Indonesian, Romanian, Ukrainian, Slovak and Bulgarian — each with a real graded deck of thousands of words aligned to JLPT / CEFR / HSK / TOPIK levels, not a demo list.
What's free, and what's Pro?
Free: every deck, spaced repetition, streaks, quizzes, and an example sentence for each word. Pro: natural human-sounding voices, unlimited AI example sentences and in-context reading, custom topic decks, and a streak shield for the day life gets in the way.
Does it work offline?
Yes — the decks and your reviews live on your phone, so learning works on a plane. AI-generated example sentences and premium voices need a connection; everything else doesn't.
How is this different from other flashcard apps?
Honest recall. The meaning stays hidden until you've tried to remember — and revealing counts as a miss, so your stats can't lie to you. Under the hood, FSRS spaced repetition schedules each word for right before you'd forget it. No leagues, no mascot guilt.
